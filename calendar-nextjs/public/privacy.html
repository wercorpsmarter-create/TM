Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 20, 2026

1. Introduction

Task Master ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our application accesses, uses, and stores your Google user data.

2. Data Access and Use

Our application requests access to your Google Calendar data (specifically, the calendar.readonly scope). We use this access strictly for the following purpose:

  • To display your upcoming calendar events on your personal dashboard alongside your tasks.
  • To help you plan your daily schedule effectively.

We do not use this data for advertising purposes.

3. Data Storage

We do not store your calendar events on our permanent servers. Calendar data is fetched in real-time or cached temporarily in your local browser storage for performance.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transfer your Google user data to any third-party tools, AI models, or advertising networks.
